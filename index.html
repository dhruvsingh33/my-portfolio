About Me

Hi, I’m a Full Stack Developer and open-source contributor with a growing focus on Blockchain and Web3 technologies. Currently pursuing my B.Tech in Computer Science and Design, I blend my foundations in software engineering with a deep curiosity for decentralized systems and future-ready tech.

I’ve contributed to major open-source projects like the Mifos X Web App, working on everything from Angular upgrades to S3 integrations and UI/UX refinements. I’m fluent with React.js, Next.js, Angular, Node.js, Tailwind CSS, and also diving deeper into Solidity, smart contract development, and decentralized architecture.

Some of my recent projects include a Decentralized Twitter clone, an NFT Marketplace, and integrations of Web3 wallets into traditional applications. These experiences are fueling my ambition to build real-world dApps that are both user-centric and technically solid.
